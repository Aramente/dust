Common questions

Is my data used to train AI models?

No. Dust has zero data retention agreements with all model providers. Your data is never used for training.

Can I switch models freely?

Yes. Dust is model-agnostic. Use GPT-5, Claude, Gemini, Mistral — switch per agent or per task, always at the best price.

Where is my data hosted?

Enterprise customers choose between US and EU data hosting. All deployments are SOC 2 Type II certified.

Do non-technical employees need to code?

No. Most agent builders at our top customers are PMs, ops leads, and domain experts. No code required to build powerful agents.
